This is a list of common user-agents for traditional search engines and generative AI crawlers, as seen in robots.txt files.

### Search Engine Crawlers (Traditional Search)
These bots are primarily used to index content for traditional search engine results pages.

User-agent: Googlebot
Operator: Google
Purpose: The main crawler for Google Search. It indexes web pages to be included in Google's search results.

User-agent: Bingbot
Operator: Microsoft
Purpose: The main crawler for Microsoft's Bing search engine.

User-agent: DuckAssistBot
Operator: DuckDuckGo
Purpose: DuckDuckGo's search crawler for indexing content.

User-agent: YouBot
Operator: You.com
Purpose: Search crawler for You.com search engine.

User-agent: TimpiBot
Operator: Timpi
Purpose: Search crawler for the Timpi decentralized search engine.

User-agent: Facebookexternalhit 
Operator: Meta
Purpose: Facebook's crawler for link previews and content indexing.

### Generative AI Crawlers (AI Training & Answers)
These are newer bots that sites may block to prevent their content from being used for AI training or in generative AI answers.

User-agent: Google-Extended
Operator: Google
Purpose: This is Google's crawler token for AI. Blocking it is intended to prevent a site's content from being used to train Google's generative models, like Gemini.
Note: According to Google, blocking Google-Extended does not prevent content from appearing in "AI Overviews," which are considered part of the main Googlebot search product.

User-agent: GPTBot
Operator: OpenAI
Purpose: This bot scrapes content specifically to train OpenAI's models, including ChatGPT.

User-agent: OAI-SearchBot
Operator: OpenAI
Purpose: Used for ChatGPT search feature. Shows webpages as links inside ChatGPT search. NOT used for model training.

User-agent: ChatGPT-User
Operator: OpenAI
Purpose: User-driven browsing from ChatGPT and Custom GPTs. Acts after a human click.

User-agent: ChatGPT-User/2.0
Operator: OpenAI
Purpose: Updated version of ChatGPT user-driven browsing agent.

User-agent: Claude-SearchBot
Operator: Anthropic
Purpose: This bot is used by the AI assistant Claude to fetch real-time information to answer user questions with citations.

User-agent: ClaudeBot
Operator: Anthropic
Purpose: Chat citation fetch for Claude AI assistant.

User-agent: anthropic-ai
Operator: Anthropic
Purpose: Bulk model training crawler for Anthropic's AI models.

User-agent: claude-web
Operator: Anthropic
Purpose: Web-focused crawl for Claude AI.

User-agent: PerplexityBot
Operator: Perplexity AI
Purpose: The web crawler for the Perplexity AI search engine. It indexes the web to provide direct, cited answers to user queries.

User-agent: Perplexity-User
Operator: Perplexity AI
Purpose: Human-triggered visit from Perplexity AI, acting on user request.

User-agent: Amazonbot
Operator: Amazon
Purpose: Amazon's web crawler for AI training and Alexa features.

User-agent: Applebot
Operator: Apple
Purpose: Apple's web crawler for Siri and Spotlight search features.

User-agent: Applebot-Extended
Operator: Apple
Purpose: Apple's extended crawler for AI training and features.

User-agent: meta-externalagent
Operator: Meta
Purpose: Meta's external agent for AI training and content crawling.

User-agent: LinkedInBot
Operator: LinkedIn
Purpose: LinkedIn's crawler for link previews and content indexing.

User-agent: Bytespider
Operator: ByteDance
Purpose: ByteDance's web crawler for AI training and search features.

User-agent: cohere-ai
Operator: Cohere
Purpose: Cohere's web crawler for AI model training.

User-agent: AI2Bot
Operator: Allen Institute for AI
Purpose: Research crawler from the Allen Institute for AI.

User-agent: CCBot
Operator: Common Crawl
Purpose: Common Crawl's bot that builds large web archives for research and AI training.

User-agent: Diffbot
Operator: Diffbot
Purpose: Automated web data extraction bot for AI and machine learning.

User-agent: omgili
Operator: Omgili
Purpose: Web crawler for content discovery and indexing.
